About

I'm a Engineering Leader who thrives at the intersection of building and leading. By day, I guide engineering teams through the full product lifecycle — architecture decisions, sprint planning, shipping features, and everything in between. By night, I'm usually prototyping the next idea.

My work spans mobile apps, backend systems, and web platforms. I care about clean architecture, developer experience, and products that solve real problems without unnecessary complexity.

Outside of my day job, I explore ideas through personal ventures like ZunicLabs and BatchBook — building things from zero to one, learning what works, and shipping what matters.
